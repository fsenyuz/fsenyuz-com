About Me

I am a Serbian‑based civil engineer with over a decade of experience in geotechnical and civil engineering. I specialise in project planning, cost control and claim management for large‑scale infrastructure projects. I earned my B.Sc. in Civil Engineering (100 % English) from Mugla Sitki Kocman University, studied abroad at West Pomeranian University of Technology in Poland and am currently completing an M.Sc. in Geotechnical Engineering. Throughout my career I’ve helped deliver high‑budget highways and bridges across Turkey, Serbia and neighbouring regions.
